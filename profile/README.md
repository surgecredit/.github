Spend dollars, keep your Bitcoin.
Surge Credit is a Bitcoin-native dollar credit line. Not a loan — a revolving credit line you control. Draw what you need, repay when you want, re-borrow without reapplying. Your Bitcoin stays in verifiable Taproot vaults on the Bitcoin blockchain. Always.
